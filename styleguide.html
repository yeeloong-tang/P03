Visual Components

Below details the colors, radius, and font family that denote the visual design of my portfolio.

Primary color is #4B09F5, denotes majority of the visual color.

Secondary color is #4B09F5, used as hover states.

The font family used throughout is called Selecta. It is a sans-serif typeface by maxitype.

Boarder radius are employed to eliminate all straight edges. 16px for image containers, and 6px for buttons.
